UNDUP - Total 12-month unduplicated headcount
12-month unduplicated headcount: 2021-22 Indicates how many individuals - all students (EFFYALEV=1,EFYTOTLT) the institution served over a 12-month period (the unduplicated headcount) Unduplicated count - The sum of students enrolled for credit with each student counted only once during the reporting period, regardless of when the student enrolled. Credit - Recognition of attendance or performance in an instructional activity (course or program) that can be applied by a recipient toward the requirements for a degree, diploma, certificate, or other formal award.
